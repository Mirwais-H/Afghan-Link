Welcome to Afghan Link

This website is for the Afghans living in Minnesota, but others are welcome to join. We provide daily and weekly news updates, valuable resources, and programs to support Afghan families in our community. Members can connect and chat with each other on our Chat Page, sharing important information and fostering community bonds. Receive Community events through our Event page. Additionally, we offer paid membership plans where you can contribute to hosting events and supporting those in need within our community.
